The Power of Shopify Plus: Scaling International Brands

Published on Jan 05, 2026 • 10 min read

Moving from Shopify Advanced to Shopify Plus is a major milestone for any e-commerce business. It unlocks a suite of tools designed for high-volume merchants, global expansion, and enterprise-level customization.

Going Global

One of the most powerful features of Shopify Plus is "Shopify Markets Pro" and the ability to run multiple localized stores under one contract. I helped a European client expand into the US market by implementing localized pricing, tax logic, and translated content while keeping their inventory synced across all regions.

Automation with Shopify Flow

We implemented automated fraud detection, customer segmentation for VIPs, and inventory alerts using Shopify Flow. This reduced the client's manual operations work by over 15 hours per week.
