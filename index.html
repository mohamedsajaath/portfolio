Services

What I do

A glimpse into my creativity exploring innovative designs, successful collaborations, and transformative eCommerce experiences.

  • Creating Shopify Liquid storefronts from scratch, whether by developing custom themes or working with existing ones. Skilled in delivering pixel-perfect implementations based on design specifications, along with building and customizing standard Shopify themes to meet project requirements.

  • Optimizing underperforming Shopify stores by identifying bottlenecks in apps and JavaScript, improving image handling, and enhancing overall performance to achieve strong speed scores across both mobile and desktop.

  • Designing Shopify stores from the ground up, tailored to your specific requirements. Initial concepts are created and presented in Figma before development, allowing for feedback and refinements early in the process. While my focus is on development, I collaborate closely with an experienced eCommerce designer to ensure your store is both visually compelling and technically sound—bringing your vision to life with precision.

  • Integrating and customizing Shopify apps to align with business needs, while researching and selecting the most suitable tools for each use case. Experienced in managing automations and leveraging Shopify Flow to streamline and optimize business processes.
